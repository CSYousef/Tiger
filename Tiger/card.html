  • Specific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Length: 2.5m (8ft)
  • Average Lifespan: 8 - 10 years
  • Habitat : rain forests, grasslands, savannas and even mangrove swamps.

The tiger has a muscular body with strong forelimbs, a large head and a tail that is about half the length of its body.
